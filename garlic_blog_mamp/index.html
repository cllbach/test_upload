It's Garlic Planting Time in Colorado!

Fall is when it happens!

October is garlic planting time. When the air grows crisp and still, and the afternoon sunlight slants the mountain shadows. When the sky is fathomless and endlessly blue and the Sandhill Cranes circle ...now's the time to get out your trowel and get started.

For those who have never indulged in the meditative ritual of garlic planting, this site answers your questions and gets you started.

Test2

Question. When should I plant?
Answer. As long as the weather is good, wait until the end of October. I usually plant around the third week.

Question. Does garlic need care over winter?
Answer. Minimum. If it's very dry, maybe a little bit of water on it, but not too much.

Question.How deep should I plant?
Answer. About the length of the garlic clove.Take a look at our diagram under "Planting tips".
